Review - Hamtam

Welcome to the review page of Group Hamtam.

Group review:

Even though our product may not have the highest vote or the best aesthetics, it still met the requirements given to us:

  • Design and fabricated a automated way to deliver messages. (Even under time constraints)

  • Able to travel a long distance (~5.8m) - based on the actual competitions

  • Able to rotate. (Using the servo motor attached at the bottom.)

    • So what can we improve on if we had more time/experience...

    - Well, we can maybe add linear control to the speed of the motors using a wireless controller and also add tilt control to the launcher. This would allow remote operation and more precise control to achieve better results.

    So what went wrong during the Try-out? And how was it resolved?

    - The diameter of our flywheel that launches the paper plane was too large, causing the rubber bands to rub against each other. To solve this, we laser cust another flywheel with a smaller diameter to allow the paper plane to have enough grip to the flywheel during launch.

    What could we have done better?

    - We could come up with a more structured plan for us to follow, so that we could create the product with better use of time. We could also have more research done so that we have a better idea on how our product will be made.
